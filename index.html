How this course works

Each session page is a complete 4-hour class: warm-up polls, teaching segments, interactive simulators, hands-on labs, an AI Spotlight, an 8-question story-problem quiz with fully explained answers, discussion prompts, a capstone checkpoint, and an exit ticket. Everything runs in a plain browser — offline, no installs, no accounts required for the in-page tools. Your quiz scores, checkboxes, and notes save automatically in your browser on each page (use the same computer & browser to keep them; the Reset button on each page clears that page only).

Throughout the course you act as an individual cloud consultant for a fictional client, Harbor Lane Books, delivering one milestone per week. AI is your study partner twice over: every session page has a "Learn it with AI" block — tutor drills tuned to that session's course objective (Socratic questioning, debates, unlimited practice quizzes) — and free AI chatbots serve as your capstone "junior analyst," with a mandatory AI Use Log where you record what you asked and what you verified. AI drafts, you decide.
